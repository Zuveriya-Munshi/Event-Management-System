About The Event

Ketan Patel's Profile

Mr. Ketan Patel has started his singing journey since 2005 in multilanguages ( Gujarati, Hindi, Sindhi & english ). He is a versatile singer who can sing in different kind of zones like Folk, Sufi, and many more. He also performs in Garba at Vadodara's well known place Bal Bhavan since 2000 and now performing at KSCA ( Karelibaug Sports & Cultural Association ) since 2011. Ketan Patel is a multi-talented Artist as he is a Musician, Programmer and Composer..
